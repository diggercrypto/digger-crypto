PARASITA ECOSYSTEM

Compliance Statement & Naming Rationale

The term PARASITA is used strictly as a metaphor to describe a self-sustaining on-chain financial mechanism. The protocol transparently derives operational capital from its own transaction volume through predefined smart-contract rules.

All mechanics, fees and treasury operations are public, immutable and deployed on the Polygon blockchain.

1. PARASITA Protocol ($PARASITA)

Core Utility – Tribute Engine

PARASITA is the deflationary backbone of the ecosystem.

  • 1.5% Treasury Fee: used for algorithmic treasury and liquidity operations.
  • 1.5% Burn: permanently removed from circulation.
