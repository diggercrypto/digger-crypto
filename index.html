About the Ecosystem

How it works — simple flow

  1. Trade happens: every PARASITA transfer triggers a 3% tax.
  2. Treasury allocation: 1.5% of each tax is sent to the protocol treasury to execute buybacks, market-making and liquidity support strategies.
  3. Burn: 1.5% is burned automatically, reducing total supply and increasing scarcity over time.
  4. Liquidity bridge: MAMACOCO provides the core paired liquidity (PARASITA/MAMACOCO) and future staking utilities.
  5. Community governance: as the protocol evolves, governance tools will be added so the community votes on treasury usage and roadmap priorities.

Why this is different

  • Self-funding: the treasury grows as trade volume grows; you don’t rely solely on initial capital.
  • Verified deflation: a burn on each tx keeps supply reducing on every trade, not just one-time burns.
  • Dual token logic: separation of treasury utility (PARASITA) and liquidity/utility token (MAMACOCO) is clearer than many single-token models.
  • Transparent and auditable: contracts are public on PolygonScan and the community can verify treasury movements.

Tokenomics & Technical Details

PARASITA: contract & mechanics

PARASITA is implemented as a standard ERC20 token on Polygon with additional transfer hooks that apply a basis-point fee on every transfer. Fees are denominated in basis points using denominator = 10000 (100 = 1.00%). The contract includes:

  • Owner/Treasury fee allocation (configurable within a secure cap).
  • Automatic burn that decreases total supply on each taxed transfer.
  • Fee exemptions for router & treasury to enable smooth liquidity operations.
  • Optional reflection mechanics in planned V2 (transparent redistribution to holders).

Contract address (PARASITA): 0x6532463D3AD92Df83A37477A54b1d990638a5980

MAMACOCO: token role

MAMACOCO acts as the primary liquidity asset and future governance token. It will be used for liquidity pairs, staking, and as a primary treasury asset for buybacks.

Contract address (MAMACOCO): 0x6C70f9870DF0A1A2C925522b548B2CD37B7C63Fd

How to buy PARASITA (quick)

  1. Install MetaMask & switch network to Polygon (Mainnet).
  2. Buy MATIC from an exchange and bridge/transfer to Polygon wallet.
  3. Go to ApeSwap/QuickSwap, select PARASITA token address and add MATIC or MAMACOCO pair.
  4. Approve token spending in MetaMask and execute the swap. Consider small slippage tolerance if needed.

If router transactions fail because of fees, ensure the router/pair has fee exemption enabled by the owner or use the guidance in our docs.

FAQ

Is the contract verified?

Yes — the source is verified on PolygonScan. Always check the address and verification badge on Polygonscan before interacting.

Can the team change fees after renounce?

If ownership is renounced, the team cannot change fees or exemptions. We recommend renounce only when all settings and treasury actions are complete.
